Overview

One unified ERP covering inventory, finance, procurement, sales, manufacturing and HR. Built on Odoo with custom modules — multi-company, multi-currency, multi-warehouse — and integrated with your accounting, e-commerce, payment and government e-invoicing systems.

02

Key Features

▸

Inventory & Warehouse

Multi-warehouse stock, lot/batch tracking, expiry, transfers, cycle counts and barcode scanning.

▸

Finance & Accounting

GL, AR, AP, multi-currency, bank reconciliation, financial statements and audit trail.

▸

Procurement

Vendor management, RFQ, purchase orders, goods receipt and three-way matching.

▸

Sales & CRM

Quotations, sales orders, invoicing, customer portal and integrated CRM pipeline.

▸

Manufacturing

BOM, work orders, MRP, routing, quality control and shop-floor data capture.

▸

E-Invoice (LHDN)

Malaysian e-invoice integration with validation, archival and consolidated submission.
