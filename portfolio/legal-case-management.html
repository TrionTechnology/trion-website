Overview

Practice management for law firms — case files, document vault, time tracking, trust accounts, invoicing, calendar, role-based access and client portal. Built for hybrid and distributed teams with cloud-first architecture and granular permissions.

02

Key Features

▸

Case Management

Full case file with parties, court dates, status, milestones and linked documents.

▸

Document Vault

Secure document store with versioning, OCR search, access control and audit log.

▸

Time Tracking

Billable hours by case and lawyer, with timesheet approval and invoice generation.

▸

Invoicing & Trust

Itemised invoices, trust account management, retainers, statements and online payments.

▸

Calendar & Reminders

Court dates, filing deadlines, auto-reminders, conflict checks across the firm.

▸

Role-Based Access

Granular permissions per role, case and document — partners, associates, paralegals.
