Overview

Production-grade loan management built for lenders, fintechs and micro-finance operators. Borrower onboarding, eKYC, AI credit scoring, approval workflow, disbursement, repayment and collections — with a full admin portal and regulatory reporting.

02

Key Features

▸

Application Portal

Borrower onboarding with eKYC, document upload, OCR and instant eligibility checks.

▸

AI Credit Scoring

Rules + machine-learning scoring with credit bureau integration and customisable risk policies.

▸

Approval Workflow

Multi-tier approval, exception handling, role-based access and full audit log.

▸

Disbursement

Bank transfer integration, FPX/DuitNow, scheduled payouts, currency control.

▸

Repayment & Collections

Schedule generation, automated reminders (SMS/email/WhatsApp), online repayment, NPL tracking.

▸

Admin Dashboard

Real-time portfolio health, NPL ratios, collection KPIs and regulatory reports.
