Overview

On-demand and scheduled wellness bookings — massage, spa, beauty, fitness, yoga and more. Customer app, therapist/practitioner app, business admin console, in-app payments, ratings, location-based matching and recurring bookings.

Key Features

▸

Service Catalogue

Configurable services with duration, pricing, prerequisites and category filters.

▸

Therapist Matching

Match by skill, distance, rating and availability — manual or automated.

▸

Bookings & Recurrence

One-off and recurring bookings, packages, cancellation rules and rescheduling.

▸

In-app Payments

Cards, wallet, packages, tipping, automatic therapist settlement.

▸

Therapist App

Schedule, navigation, customer notes, earnings, ratings, KYC and document upload.

▸

Business Console

Multi-outlet admin, staff onboarding, payouts, performance reports.
