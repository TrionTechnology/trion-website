Overview

A complete ride-hailing / driver-for-hire / transport platform. Real-time GPS tracking, driver matching, route optimisation, dynamic pricing, in-app payments, ratings and a fleet admin console with city-level operations control.

02

Key Features

▸

Real-time GPS Tracking

Live driver and trip tracking on map with smooth interpolation and offline tolerance.

▸

Driver Matching

Geo-spatial driver matching with surge pricing, ETA estimation and re-assignment logic.

▸

Route Optimisation

Google Maps / Mapbox routing, multi-stop optimisation, traffic-aware ETAs.

▸

In-app Payments

Cashless trips, multiple payment methods, in-app wallet, automatic driver settlement.

▸

Driver App

Native driver app with online/offline mode, earnings, ratings, document upload.

▸

Fleet Operations Console

Multi-city control, driver onboarding, KYC, payouts, disputes and dashboards.
