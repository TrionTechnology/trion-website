Overview

Complete POS for restaurants and retail — multi-outlet, real-time inventory sync, kitchen display systems, QR table ordering, e-menu, customer loyalty, online ordering and integrated payment (cards, e-wallets, FPX, GrabPay, Boost, ShopeePay).

02

Key Features

▸

Tablet & Desktop POS

Fast tablet and desktop POS with split bills, table layout, modifiers and offline mode.

▸

Kitchen Display (KDS)

Real-time order routing to kitchen stations with course timing and order status.

▸

QR Table Ordering

Guests scan, browse e-menu, order, pay — no waiting, no staff bottleneck.

▸

Inventory & Recipes

Ingredient-level stock control, recipe management, auto-deduction with sales.

▸

Loyalty & E-Wallet

Member tiers, points, vouchers, push promos, integrated e-wallet for repeat business.

▸

Payments

Cards, FPX, DuitNow QR, GrabPay, Boost, ShopeePay, TnG, cash, split tender.
