Overview

Complete HR and payroll management designed for Malaysian SMEs and enterprises. Attendance with mobile clock-in, leave with approval workflow, payroll with EPF/SOCSO/EIS/PCB calculations, expense claims, employee self-service portal and management dashboards.

02

Key Features

▸

Attendance & Time

Mobile clock-in with geo-fencing, QR check-in, shift schedules and overtime tracking.

▸

Leave Management

Multi-type leave with quotas, approval chains, calendars, leave-balance dashboards.

▸

Payroll Processing

EPF, SOCSO, EIS, HRDF, PCB calculations, payslip PDF, bank file export, year-end forms.

▸

Expense Claims

Receipt upload, OCR, multi-level approval, reimbursement integration, audit trail.

▸

Employee Self-Service

View payslips, apply leave, submit claims, update profile and access training records.

▸

Compliance Reports

Form E, EA, CP8D, statutory submissions and LHDN e-invoicing ready.
