01

Overview

Inventory and supplier management for distributors, e-commerce and multi-outlet retailers. Real-time stock across warehouses, purchase order workflows, supplier portal, barcode and lot tracking, demand forecasting and integrations with accounting and e-commerce.

02

Key Features

▸

Real-time Inventory

Stock across warehouses and outlets, instant updates, transfer workflows, cycle counts.

▸

Purchase Orders

RFQ, PO generation, approval workflow, goods receipt and three-way matching.

▸

Supplier Portal

Suppliers log in to view POs, confirm delivery, upload invoices and respond to RFQs.

▸

Barcode & Lot Tracking

Barcode/QR scanning, batch and serial tracking, expiry alerts, FIFO/LIFO.

▸

Demand Forecasting

Sales-driven reorder points, auto-PO triggers, seasonality and trend analysis.

▸

Integrations

Sync with Shopify / WooCommerce / Lazada / Shopee / AutoCount / QuickBooks.
