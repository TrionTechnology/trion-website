Overview

End-to-end project management — Kanban boards, Gantt timelines, resource allocation, budget tracking, client portal and real-time collaboration. Built for agencies, contractors and project-driven teams that need more than off-the-shelf SaaS.

Key Features

▸

Tasks & Kanban

Drag-drop kanban with checklists, comments, attachments, dependencies and watchers.

▸

Gantt Timeline

Interactive Gantt with milestones, critical path, baselines and progress tracking.

Capacity planning, utilisation reports, conflict detection and team workload balancing.

Project budgets, cost categories, time logs, profitability by project and client.
