Project Overview

DDDrive is a comprehensive driver-for-hire services platform that connects users with trusted drivers for safe transportation. The platform emphasizes safety, reliability, and convenience through advanced technology and user-centric design.

Challenge

Creating a reliable driver dispatch system that could handle real-time matching, secure payments, driver onboarding, and rating systems while maintaining scalability for multi-city deployment.

Solution

We developed a robust platform with real-time driver dispatch, in-app wallet integration, comprehensive driver onboarding system, and scalable backend architecture supporting multi-city operations.
