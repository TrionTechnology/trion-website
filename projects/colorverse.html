Project Overview

Colorverse is a Web3-based Social-Fi platform designed for creators, with features similar to Patreon, enabling creators to publish content and interact with fans, while earning incentives through Web3 technology and token economics.

Challenge

Creating a seamless bridge between Web2 and Web3 experiences while maintaining user-friendly interfaces and implementing complex smart contract automation for revenue sharing across multiple blockchain networks.

Solution

We developed a hybrid platform that combines traditional social media features with cutting-edge Web3 technology, including gasless NFT minting, multi-chain support, and automated smart contract revenue distribution.
