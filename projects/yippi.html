Project Overview

Yippi is a social platform focused on the Asian region (primarily China, Indonesia, Malaysia, and Vietnam), covering several core social app features such as live streaming, short videos, e-commerce, instant messaging, mini programs, and loyalty programs.

Challenge

Building a scalable social media platform that could handle massive concurrent users across multiple Asian markets while maintaining smooth live streaming performance and ensuring seamless cross-border content delivery.

Solution

We developed a comprehensive social media super app with advanced CDN optimization, real-time video transcoding, and highly scalable architecture supporting tens of thousands of concurrent users.
